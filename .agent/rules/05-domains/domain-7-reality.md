## Domain 7: Reality Rules Constraints

### Canonical Ground Truth

**Paradox Mechanics:**

*   Reality contradictions create paradoxes
*   CPS (Cognitive Paradox Syndrome) from paradox exposure
*   Data-Wraiths are psychic paradox manifestations
*   Blight = corrupted Aether spreading paradox contamination

**Ginnungagap Glitch Effects:**

*   Reality substrate corruption
*   Systems behave contradictorily
*   Deterministic logic breaks down
*   Continuous reality instability (not one-time event)

**Runic Blight Propagation:**

*   Spreads through inscriptions and Aetheric connections
*   Corrupts functional systems into paradox states
*   No "cure" exists (containment only)

### Audit Checklist

*   \[ \] Paradox mechanics consistent with canonical rules?
*   \[ \] CPS accumulation follows established progression?
*   \[ \] Data-Wraith manifestations use canonical mechanics?
*   \[ \] Blight corruption spread follows established patterns?
*   \[ \] Ginnungagap Glitch effects appropriately described?
*   \[ \] No deus ex machina solutions to paradox problems?
*   \[ \] Reality instability ongoing (not resolved)?

### Common Violations

❌ Paradox "cures" or easy solutions
❌ CPS recovery without consequences
❌ Blight containment too easy
❌ Unexplained supernatural effects
❌ Reality contradictions without paradox consequences

### Green Flags

✅ CPS as progressive, irreversible condition
✅ Blight as persistent threat
✅ Paradox exposure creates consequences
✅ Reality instability ongoing
