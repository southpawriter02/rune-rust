## Summary

Implements the complete Midgard realm biome definition with its four canonical sub-zones from the lore document, faction spawn pools per zone, environmental condition overrides, and property overrides. Adds the `FactionPools` and `Description` properties to `RealmBiomeZone`, updates the JSON configuration, and schema.
