# Codex Entry: Ash-Crow (Soot-Feathered Harbinger)

> *Jötun-Reader Field Notation: The following entry synthesizes five evidence fragments recovered between 779-783 PG. Unlock thresholds defined per Bestiary protocol. Respiratory protection mandatory in confirmed territories.*

---

## 1.0 Overview

The **Ash-Crow**—known to the Hearth-Clans as the *Soot-Feathered Omen* and designated "Screaming Vanguard" in Scriptorium field reports—represents a profound example of post-Glitch mutagenic adaptation. These large corvids, indigenous to the **Ashfall Drifts** of Muspelheim, have evolved to weaponize their environment: their ragged feathers perpetually absorb toxic particulates from the **Surtr Forges**, which can be violently expelled as a corrosive, blinding cloud.

More troubling than individual specimens is their ecological role. Ash-Crows hunt in shrieking flocks of 12-40 individuals, their cacophonous calls creating an auditory veil that masks the approach of larger predators—most notably the subterranean **Ash-Wyrms**. This symbiotic predation pattern makes any Ash-Crow encounter a potential indicator of far greater danger.

---

## 2.0 Physical Characteristics

Field observations document a large corvid of considerable size:

| Characteristic | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Dimensions** | Body length 50-70 cm; wingspan 110-140 cm; weight 1.2-2.0 kg |
| **Plumage** | Matte black base coloration with blue-green iridescent sheen (visible only when ash coating minimal); feathers perpetually caked in grey-to-rust toxic ash; individual barbs frayed from continuous corrosive exposure |
| **Adaptations** | Beak and talons show mineral hardening from environmental absorption; eyes retain corvid intelligence with amber-to-red iris coloration |
| **Detection Signatures** | Harsh, metallic cawing (2-4 kHz range); visible ash trails during flight; distinctive acrid-metallic scent |

## 3.0 Corrosive Ash Weaponization

The Ash-Crow's primary threat derives from its feather-borne toxin delivery system. Forensic analysis (Report ID: ASHC-77-Muspelheim) confirms:

| Property | Analysis |
|----------|----------|
| **Composition** | Complex bio-mineral particulates; pH 3.2-4.1 (acidic); contains trace heavy metals and crystalline silicates |
| **Deployment** | Violent shaking releases dense blinding cloud |
| **Effects** | Acute irritation; temporary blindness (8-15 minutes); localized chemical burns on exposed skin; severe respiratory distress from inhalation |
| **Bio-Aetheric Contamination** | Ash coating carries low-level **Runic Blight** signature; cumulative exposure exceeding 30 minutes may induce minor **Psychic Stress** |

| **Vanguard Function** | Act as "eyes and ears" for **Ash-Wyrms**, flushing and disorienting prey, guiding larger predators to weakened targets |
