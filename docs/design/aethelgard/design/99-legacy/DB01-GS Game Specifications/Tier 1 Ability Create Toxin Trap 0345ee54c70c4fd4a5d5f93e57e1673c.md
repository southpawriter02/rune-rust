## Core Identity

**Create Toxin Trap** lets you weaponize the environment. You distill Blighted essence into concentrated hazard zones—pools of caustic vapor, tripwire-triggered spore bursts, or seeping pits of entropic sludge. Enemies who enter suffer.

---

## Mechanics

### Cost

20 Stamina | **Standard Action** | **Range:** 6m

### Effect

- Place a **[Toxin Trap]** terrain hazard in target zone (2m radius)
- Trap is **Hidden** until triggered (enemy WITS vs. your FINESSE to detect)
- **Trigger:** Enemy enters zone
- **Effect:** Apply **2 stacks [Poisoned]** + create **[Toxic Cloud]** (3 turns)
- [Toxic Cloud]: Enemies entering/starting turn take **1d6 damage**

### Rank Progression

| Rank | PP Cost | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 3 | 2 stacks [Poisoned] + 1d6 cloud damage |
| 2 | 20 | 3 stacks [Poisoned]; 15 Stamina; cloud lasts 4 turns |
| 3 | 35 | 3 stacks + [Slowed] for 1 turn; cloud deals 1d8 damage |

## Tactical Applications

**Ambush Setup:**

```jsx
Out of combat: Place traps at chokepoints
Enemy patrol approaches: Hidden trap triggers
Result: Pre-combat damage + poison stacks before initiative
```

**Zone Denial:**

```jsx
Combat: Place trap behind enemy backline
Enemy retreat: Walks into trap
Result: Punish disengagement, control positioning
```

**Synergies:**

- **Blighted Symbiosis:** Your own toxic clouds heal you
- **Corruption Catalyst:** Poison damage boosted by your Corruption
- **Miasmic Shroud:** Multiple overlapping hazard zones
