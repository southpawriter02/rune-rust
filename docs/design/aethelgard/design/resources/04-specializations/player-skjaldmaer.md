# The Skjaldmær — Bastion of Coherence

> *"This is my shield. This is my clan. This reality will break before I do."*
> — Skjaldmær oath, traditional

---

## Overview

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Archetype** | Warrior |
| **Role** | Quintessential Tank, Sanity Anchor, Defensive Bulwark |
| **Path Type** | Coherent |
| **Primary Attribute** | STURDINESS |
| **Secondary Attribute** | WILL |
| **Resource System** | Stamina |
| **Trauma Economy Risk** | Low |

In a world where reality itself is a glitching, corrupted program, the **Skjaldmær** is more than a wall of steel.

She is a **bastion of coherence**.

The Skjaldmær's role has evolved beyond merely protecting the body to the far more critical task of shielding the very **sanity** of her allies. She stands against the Runic Blight not with complex understanding, but with an unyielding, simple, and profound truth: her shield is a grounding rod, her will is a firewall, and her presence is a kernel of stable reality in a universe of paradox.

Her saga is not just written in the blows she blocks, but in the **minds she keeps from shattering**.

## Core Philosophy: The Unbreachable Wall

### The Shield as Identity

| Principle | Expression |
|-----------|------------|
| **Physical Truth** | The shield is an undeniable statement of reality in a world of glitches |
| **Will Made Manifest** | Mental fortitude channels through the shield as metaphysical protection |
| **Clan Before Self** | Protection of others is the Skjaldmær's purpose and identity |
| **Coherence as Weapon** | Simple, clear reality pushes back against paradox |

The Skjaldmær does not seek to understand the Runic Blight. She does not debate its nature or search for its cure. She simply **refuses to break**.

Her power comes from indomitable **Will**, channeled through her shield into a projection of pure, unwavering coherence. In a world where corrupted data screams endlessly and reality fractures at the edges, the Skjaldmær is a point of absolute stability—a living anchor to which sanity can cling.

> *"Her power comes from her indomitable Will, which she channels into her shield, transforming it from a simple piece of armor into a grounding rod—a metaphysical firewall against the eternal psychic scream of the Great Silence."*

### The Three Pillars

| Pillar | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **The Unbreachable Wall** | Unparalleled physical defense through shield mastery and Soak |
| **The Aegis of the Clan** | Protection extending beyond self through taunts and defensive buffs |
| **The Bastion of Sanity** | Psychic Stress mitigation—the party's sanity anchor |

The third pillar—**Bastion of Sanity**—is what separates the Skjaldmær from ordinary tanks in other settings. Many abilities are designed to absorb, redirect, or reduce incoming Psychic Stress, making the Skjaldmær an indispensable component for any expedition into Blight-saturated ruins.

This transforms the tank role from "meat shield" to **reality anchor**. The Skjaldmær protects not just HP, but sanity—the game's most valuable resource.

## Gameplay Fantasy

| Aspect | Description |

Playing a Skjaldmær is about understanding **threat priority**, positioning to maximize protection, and managing both physical and mental resources. You are the party's immovable anchor—the calm center of the storm around which battle rages.

Your personal damage output is very low. Your contribution is measured in **what doesn't happen**: allies who don't die, minds that don't break, formations that don't collapse.

> *"Her saga is not just written in the blows she blocks, but in the minds she keeps from shattering."*

## Abilities: The Protector's Oath

### Tier 1 — Foundational Resolve

| Ability | Type | Cost | Effect |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **Sanctified Resolve** | Passive | — | +1 die to WILL Resolve Checks vs. [Fear] and Psychic Stress |
| **Shield Bash** | Active | 40 Stamina | Low Physical damage; [Staggered] on Solid/Critical Success |
| **Oath of the Protector** | Active | 35 Stamina | Single ally gains +2 Soak, +1 die vs. Psychic Stress (2 turns) |

**Sanctified Resolve** establishes the Skjaldmær's mental fortress identity. Before she can protect others, she must be unshakeable herself. This passive grants permanent bonus dice to resist Fear and Psychic Stress—the foundation upon which all other abilities build.

**Shield Bash** is a brutal, declarative statement of reality. Low damage, but the [Staggered] effect provides crowd control and establishes the shield as a weapon, not just a defensive tool.

**Oath of the Protector** is the Skjaldmær's core single-target protection ability. The dual physical/mental protection—Soak bonus plus Psychic Stress resistance—establishes the unique identity that separates this specialization from generic tanks.

### Tier 2 — Advanced Guardianship

| Ability | Type | Cost | Effect |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **Guardian's Taunt** | Active | 30 Stamina | Taunt all Front Row enemies (2 rounds); self gains Psychic Stress |
| **Shield Wall** | Active | 45 Stamina | Self + adjacent Front Row allies gain +3 Soak, Push/Pull immunity, +1 die vs. Psychic Stress (2 turns) |
| **Interposing Shield** | Reaction | 25 Stamina | Redirect Critical Hit targeting adjacent ally to self; take 50% damage |

**Guardian's Taunt** is a projection of pure, coherent will into the static of the battlefield—a signal of sanity so strong that even the most maddened creatures are drawn to it. The Psychic Stress cost reflects the taxing nature of drawing the world's trauma to herself; this is selfless sacrifice, not free aggro generation.

**Shield Wall** is the formation-based defensive cooldown—a powerful group buff that rewards positioning and party coordination. The triple protection (Physical + Positional + Mental) establishes the Skjaldmær as the anchor point for coordinated defensive play.

> *"The Skjaldmær plants her feet, creating a temporary bastion of physical and metaphysical stability. Allies instinctively rally to this anchor of coherence."*

**Interposing Shield** is the classic tank reaction—the clutch save that rewards awareness and positioning. When devastation is about to land on an ally, the Skjaldmær throws herself into the path.

### Tier 3 — Mastery of the Bulwark

| Ability | Type | Cost | Effect |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **Implacable Defense** | Active | 40 Stamina | Immunity to [Stun], [Staggered], [Knocked Down], [Fear], [Disoriented] (3 turns) |
| **Aegis of the Clan** | Passive | — | Auto-applies Oath of the Protector (free) when ally's Psychic Stress enters High threshold |

**Implacable Defense** is the ultimate stability cooldown. For three turns, the Skjaldmær achieves perfect focus—an unmovable object immune to both physical control effects and mental assault. This ensures she can hold the line under any conditions, maintaining protection when it matters most.

**Aegis of the Clan** is the automatic clutch save. When an ally's mind begins to fracture—when their Psychic Stress enters the danger zone—the Skjaldmær's protective instincts trigger immediately. No action required, no cost expended. The sight of a struggling ally's fracturing mind triggers an overwhelming need to protect them.

### Capstone — Bastion of Sanity

| Component | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Passive Aura** | Front Row allies gain +1 WILL, -10% ambient Psychic Stress gain |
| **Reaction** | Once per combat: prevent ally Trauma; absorb massive Psychic Stress + 1 Corruption |

The capstone **Bastion of Sanity** transforms the Skjaldmær into a living Runic Anchor—a walking kernel of stable code. Her presence is a constant, calming hum of coherence in a world of static.

The **Passive Aura** provides always-on protection for allies in her row—increased WILL and reduced environmental Psychic Stress accumulation. Simply standing near the Skjaldmær makes the world a little less maddening.

The **Reaction** is the ultimate expression of selfless protection. When an ally would gain permanent Trauma from a Breaking Point—when their mind is about to shatter—the Skjaldmær can absorb the psychic backlash. The ally is saved from permanent scarring. The Skjaldmær takes massive Psychic Stress and 1 Corruption.

> *"Once per combat, when an ally would gain a permanent Trauma from reaching maximum Psychic Stress, the Skjaldmær can trigger this ability: The ally does not gain the Trauma. The Skjaldmær takes a massive amount of Psychic Stress and 1 Runic Blight Corruption, absorbing the psychic backlash to save her comrade from a permanent scar."*

This is the Skjaldmær's ultimate sacrifice—literally taking a mental bullet for an ally, preventing permanent consequences at great personal cost.

## Systemic Integration

### Trauma Economy Interaction

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Path Type** | Coherent (no negative Trauma interactions) |
| **Self-Inflicted Risk** | Low (only Guardian's Taunt and capstone have Psychic costs) |
| **Protective Role** | Actively mitigates party Psychic Stress through multiple abilities |
| **Ultimate Sacrifice** | Capstone reaction absorbs Trauma/Corruption to save allies |

The Skjaldmær is a **Coherent** specialization—her power comes from discipline, will, and selfless dedication to her clan. She has no heretical bargains, no corruption-based mechanics, no self-destructive resource systems.

This makes her the natural complement to high-risk party members. A Berserkr can rage freely knowing the Skjaldmær will absorb the consequences. An Echo-Caller can push their limits knowing the Skjaldmær will catch them when they fall.

### Situational Power Profile

| Condition | Performance |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Optimal** | High-damage encounters; Blight-heavy environments; protecting fragile allies |
| **Strong** | Formation-based combat; coordinated parties; boss fights |
| **Weak** | Solo play; low-threat encounters; ranged-heavy parties |
| **Hard Countered By** | Armor-piercing attacks; crowd control preventing reactions; taunt immunity |

### Party Synergies

| Ally | Synergy |
|------|---------|
| **Berserkr / Vargr-Born** | Protects reckless melee fighters, enabling aggressive play |
| **Bone-Setter / Skald** | Forms complete defense triangle (damage prevention + healing + Stress prevention) |
| **Jötun-Reader / Echo-Caller** | Critical for managing self-inflicted Psychic Stress |
| **Formation builds** | Shield Wall maximizes value with coordinated positioning |

The Skjaldmær's **positive synergies** center on protecting high-risk party members. She enables aggressive playstyles by absorbing the consequences that would otherwise punish them.

Her **negative synergies** are with solo/mobile strikers who don't benefit from front-row protection, and with other tanks who create role redundancy.

## Summary

The Skjaldmær is the **party's anchor**—both physically and metaphysically.

In a world where reality itself glitches and the eternal scream of the Runic Blight breaks minds as easily as blows break bones, the Skjaldmær offers something irreplaceable: **coherence**. Her shield is not just steel. It is a statement of undeniable reality, a firewall against paradox, a kernel of stable code in a corrupted program.

Her gameplay reflects this identity. She does not deal damage—she prevents it. She does not kill enemies—she draws their attention and absorbs their fury. She does not understand the Blight—she simply refuses to let it break her or those she protects.

The Skjaldmær's three pillars define her role:

**The Unbreachable Wall** makes her nearly impossible to kill through conventional damage. Her Soak is unmatched, her resilience legendary.

**The Aegis of the Clan** extends her protection to allies. Her taunts draw enemy attention; her buffs grant temporary fortification; her reactions intercept killing blows.

**The Bastion of Sanity** is what makes her unique in Aethelgard. In a setting where Psychic Stress is the most dangerous resource and Trauma is the most feared consequence, the Skjaldmær actively mitigates both. Her aura reduces ambient Stress. Her abilities grant resistance. Her capstone can absorb permanent Trauma at the cost of her own stability.

This is the Skjaldmær's ultimate sacrifice: she will take the mental blow meant for you. She will absorb the scream that would shatter your mind. She will carry the Corruption that would have broken you.

Because that is what she was trained to do.

That is what she chooses to do.

*This is my shield.*

*This is my clan.*

*This reality will break before I do.*
