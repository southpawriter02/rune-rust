## Thematic Description

> *"A finish begins the next approach; a cleared lane grants immediate movement to the next fault line."*
> 

No Quarter is the Strandhögg's **kill-chain mobility**. Each enemy you drop opens a path to the next target—you flow from finish to finish without pause.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Trigger**: When you **kill an enemy**
- **Effect**: Immediately move up to **4 tiles** (no action cost)

### Rank 2

- **Movement**: Up to **6 tiles** (up from 4)
- **Safety**: Movement does **not provoke** opportunity attacks
- **PP Cost**: 5

### Rank 3

- **Bonus Damage**: After kill-movement, your next attack this turn deals **+3d6 damage**
- **PP Cost**: 5

## Synergies

### Internal (Strandhögg Tree)

- **Savage Harvest**: Kill → No Quarter movement → repeat
- **Harrier's Whirlwind**: Strike, kill, free move, Whirlwind to safety
- **Riptide**: Multiple kills = multiple No Quarter triggers

### External (Party Composition)

- **Damage dealers**: Help secure kills for triggers
- **Controllers**: Keep enemies clustered for chain kills
- **Ranged specialists**: You close gaps they can't

---

## Tactical Applications

### The Kill Chain

1. Kill Enemy A
2. No Quarter: Free 4-6 tile movement
3. Engage Enemy B
4. Kill Enemy B
5. No Quarter: Move to Enemy C
6. R3: +3d6 on each follow-up attack

### Riptide Synergy

- Riptide grants extra Standard Action
- Multiple kills per turn
- Multiple No Quarter triggers
- Massive battlefield coverage

### Rank 3: Momentum Damage

- Every kill = +3d6 on next attack
- Chain kills = sustained bonus damage
- Encourages finishing multiple enemies
