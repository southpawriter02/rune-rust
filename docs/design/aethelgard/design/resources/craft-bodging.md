## Failed Craft Consequences (Expanded)

| Failure Margin | Result | Description |
|----------------|--------|-------------|
| 1–2 | **Wasted Materials** | Components ruined; can retry tomorrow |
| 3–4 | **Locked Out** | Cannot attempt this recipe for 24 hours; materials lost |
| 5–6 | **Backfire** | Device explodes; 2d10 damage to crafter |
| 7–8 | **Shrapnel Spray** | 3d10 damage in 3m radius; potential injuries to bystanders |
| 9+ | **Catastrophic Failure** | Major explosion; 4d10 damage; workspace destroyed |
| **Critical Failure (Spirit Plate)** | **Wild Surge** | Aetheric discharge; roll on instability table; +2 Corruption |
