## Thematic Description

> *"Strike and vanish on the same breath; repositions without inviting a counter."*
> 

Harrier's Whirlwind is the Strandhögg's **strike-and-vanish technique**. You attack and immediately disengage, flowing to a new position before your opponent can react.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Cost**: 45 Stamina + 20 Pace
- **Target**: Single enemy (melee range)
- **Attack**: FINESSE vs target's Defense
- **Damage**: 3d10 Physical
- **Reposition**: After attack, immediately move up to **3 tiles** without provoking opportunity attacks

### Rank 2

- **Damage**: 4d10 Physical (up from 3d10)
- **Reposition**: Up to **4 tiles** (up from 3)
- **PP Cost**: 4

### Rank 3

- **Kill Refund**: If attack **kills target**, Pace cost is **refunded**
- **PP Cost**: 4

## Synergies

### External (Party Composition)

- **Ranged specialists**: Reposition to flanking angles
- **Tanks**: Disengage to safety behind front line
- **Controllers**: Exit danger zones after strike

## Tactical Applications

### Hit-and-Run Protocol

1. Build Pace via movement/attacks
2. Engage isolated target
3. Harrier's Whirlwind (strike + escape)
4. Reposition behind tank or to next target
5. Repeat

### Rank 3: Kill Chain Efficiency

- Kill target → 20 Pace refunded
- Combined with No Quarter free movement
- Net positive Pace on kills
- Sustain aggression indefinitely
