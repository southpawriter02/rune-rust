## Executive Summary

v0.19.3 implements the extreme temperature realms: Muspelheim (the eternal meltdown) and Niflheim (the frozen tomb). These realms are **Incompatible** and cannot neighbor directly. This version introduces IntenseHeat and ExtremeCold environmental mechanics with full zone configurations from the canonical lore documents.

### Muspelheim Environmental Mechanics (Lore §4)

#### IntenseHeat (§4.1)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Base DC** | 12 (STURDINESS) |
| **Base Damage** | 2d6 Fire |
| **Frequency** | Per Turn |
| **Zone Modifiers** | Hearths: DC 6, Slag: DC 14, Gjöllflow: DC 16, Ashfall: DC 12 |

**Zone-Specific Damage:**

| Zone | DC | Damage |
|------|-----|--------|
| Hearth Interior | 6 | 1d6 |
| Hearth Perimeter | 12 | 2d6 |
| Slag Wastes | 14 | 2d8 |
| Gjöllflow Proximity | 16 | 3d6 |
| Forge Core Approach | 20 | 4d6 |

**Mitigations:**
- Fire Resistance reduces damage
- Cooling equipment provides DC reduction (-2 to -4)
- Hearth shelter negates ambient condition
- Dvergr thermal suits provide +4 to saves

#### Brittleness Mechanic (§4.2)

Counter-mechanic rewarding Ice-specialized builds:

```
Ice Attack → Fire Resistant Target → [Brittle] Debuff Applied
                                    ↓
                            Physical Vulnerability (+50%)
```

| **Effect** | Visibility -2 to ranged beyond 10m |

#### Ice-Debt Mechanic (§4.2)

Unique to Hvergelmir's Maw:

```
Each Turn in Hvergelmir → +1 Ice-Debt
                         ↓
Ice-Debt 3: [Slowed] status
Ice-Debt 5: [Frozen] status (incapacitated)
Ice-Debt 7: Preserved (death, but...)
```

**Clearing Ice-Debt:**
- Heat source: -1 per turn adjacent
- Fire spell: -2 per casting
- Exit zone: -1 per turn outside
- Hearth-Clan warming kit: -3 immediate
