# v0.20.4b Changelog — Myrk-gengr Tier 2 Abilities

## Added

- **Umbral Strike** — Shadow-infused melee attack (2 AP, 15 Essence)
    - Weapon damage + 2d6 shadow damage from DimLight or Darkness
    - Critical hit: +1d6 bonus shadow damage
    - Clone consumption: advantage + 1d4 bonus shadow damage
    - Corruption risk evaluated per strike

- **Shadow Clone** — Illusory duplicate creation (3 AP, 20 Essence)
    - 1 HP, DC 14 Perception to detect, 1 minute duration
    - Maximum 2 active clones simultaneously
    - Four behavior modes: Mirror, Independent, Decoy, Static
    - Consumable by Umbral Strike for advantage

- **Void Touched** — Passive resistance
    - Halves incoming Aetheric damage (floor division)
    - Grants advantage on saves vs. Aetheric effects

- **Domain Layer**
    - `CloneBehavior` enum (Mirror, Independent, Decoy, Static)
    - `ShadowClone` value object with lifecycle management
    - `DamageBreakdown` value object for multi-type damage
    - `UmbralStrikeResult` value object with hit/miss/failure

- **Application Layer**
    - `MyrkgengrTier2AbilityService` with PP cost tracking
    - Full structured logging for all ability operations

- **Configuration**
    - Tier 2 block added to `specializations.json`
    - 3 ability definitions with narrative templates added to `specialization-abilities.json`

- **Testing**
    - 19 unit tests in `MyrkgengrTier2AbilityServiceTests`
