## Pre-Glitch Origins: The Unyielding Sanctuary

### Original Function

In the Age of Forging, Algiz (ᛉ) served as the root function for creating fields of repulsion, sanctuary, and non-interference. Unlike Nauthiz (ᚾ), which established hard, physical barriers or kinetic constraints, Algiz created "soft, conceptual" protection—manipulating perception and interaction at a fundamental Aetheric level rather than through brute force.

| Operational Theater | Function |
|---------------------|----------|
| **IFF Systems** | Identification-Friend-or-Foe discrimination; authorized users perceived as "background" |
| **Stealth Protocols** | Conceptual concealment; subjects not merely camouflaged but "absent" from awareness |
| **Personal Wards** | Temporary localized sanctuaries; deflection of unwanted attention |
| **Facility Protection** | Architectural integration; zones of "engineered silence" |
| **Psychic Shielding** | Protection from chaotic Aetheric noise; sanity preservation |

### The Soft Barrier

Algiz's distinctive characteristic was its operation through perception rather than force:

| System | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Conceptual Repulsion** | Command: "Do not perceive me" / "Do not interact with me" |
| **IFF Integration** | Bio-Aetheric signature nullification for authorized personnel |
| **Visibility Masking** | Subjects harmonized into environmental white noise |
| **Interaction Prohibition** | Fundamental Aetheric resonance manipulation |
| **Sanctuary Envelope** | Localized zones of perfect non-interference |
| **Noise Dampening** | Protection from ambient Aetheric/psychic interference |

**The Silent Rooms:**

In sensitive research facilities—the reality-stabilization laboratories of Alfheim, the private quarters of Aesir elites on Asgard—Algiz arrays were woven into the very architecture. These created zones of "perfect, engineered silence" and conceptual inviolability, protecting both delicate control systems and the sanity of technicians from chaotic Aetheric or psychic noise. The Algiz ward was the ultimate assurance: safety, privacy, non-interference.
