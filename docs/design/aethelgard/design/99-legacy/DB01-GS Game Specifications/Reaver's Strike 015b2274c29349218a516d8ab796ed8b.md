## Thematic Description

> *"A swift test-stroke to probe guard and set rhythm; establishes pressure without over-commitment."*
> 

Reaver's Strike is the Strandhögg's **core melee attack**. A probing strike that builds momentum without overextending—the opening move of every raid.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Cost**: 30 Stamina
- **Target**: Single enemy (melee range)
- **Attack**: FINESSE vs target's Defense
- **Damage**: 2d8 Physical
- **Pace Generation**: On hit, generate **10 Pace**

### Rank 2

- **Damage**: 3d8 Physical (up from 2d8)
- **Pace Generation**: 15 Pace (up from 10)
- **PP Cost**: 3

### Rank 3

- **Bonus Damage**: If target has any negative status effect, deal **+2d6 bonus damage**
- **PP Cost**: 3

## Synergies

### Internal (Strandhögg Tree)

- **Harrier's Alacrity**: Starting Pace + Reaver's Strike = fast threshold reach
- **Vicious Flank**: R3 exploits same status effect targeting
- **Tidal Rush**: Allied CC triggers additional Pace

### External (Party Composition)

- **Controllers**: Any debuff enables R3 bonus damage
- **Damage dealers**: Reliable Pace builder
- **Mobile allies**: Coordinate strikes on debuffed targets

---

## Tactical Applications

### Pace Building Loop

1. Move (5 Pace/tile)
2. Reaver's Strike (+10/15 Pace)
3. Tidal Rush triggers (+5/8/12 per ally debuff)
4. Repeat until threshold reached
5. Execute with Savage Harvest or Riptide

### Rank 3: Opportunist

- +2d6 vs debuffed targets
- Synergizes with Dread Charge ([Disoriented])
- Rewards coordinated team play
