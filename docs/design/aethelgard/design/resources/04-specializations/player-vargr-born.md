# The Vargr-Born — The Uncorrupted Echo

> *"You hear the howl and you think: wolf. You think: beast. You think: mindless hunger. You are wrong. My howl is not hunger—it is clarity. It is the sound of the old world, the pure world, the world before the Glitch. To the corrupted, to the Blighted, to the things whose minds are already full of static—my howl is a blade of silence that cuts through their chaos. I am not a monster. I am the cure. The **Vargr-Born** is the Warrior specialization that embodies the philosophy of the **uncorrupted, primal echo**.[^]

They are not heretics who channel the Blight, nor disciplined soldiers who rely on formation and steel. Their bloodline has become fused with a sliver of the world's original, stable source code—an ancient, predatory "wolf" spirit that is pure, logical, and utterly savage. This makes them a living counter-frequency to the Great Silence.

To choose the Vargr-Born is to embrace the fantasy of the **feral brawler and the psychological predator**. Your saga is written in the bleeding wounds you inflict and the terror you sow with your howl. You are the old world's antibody, a savage cure for a modern disease.

## Gameplay Fantasy

### Player Experience

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Core Fantasy** | Feral brawler; apex predator; psychological warfare specialist |
| **Gameplay Feel** | Aggressive, relentless, self-sufficient melee damage dealer |
| **Success Metric** | Bleeding wounds; building Fury; devastating finishers |
| **Decision Space** | When to apply bleeds; when to spend Fury; who to hunt |

Playing a Vargr-Born is about **predatory patience followed by explosive violence**.[^]

Your operational loop is:
1. **Stalk**: Use Primal Senses to track and identify priority prey
2. **Wound**: Apply [Bleeding] with Savage Claws to open the hunt
3. **Build**: Generate Feral Fury from successful attacks and bleed ticks
4. **Break**: Use Terrifying Howl to inflict [Feared] and Psychic Stress
5. **Kill**: Spend Fury on Go for the Throat for devastating finisher damage
6. **Ascend**: When bloodied, become even more dangerous via Wounded Animal's Ferocity

> *"The hunt has a rhythm. First, I watch. I learn the prey's patterns, its weaknesses, its fear. Then I strike—not to kill, but to wound. The bleeding starts. The prey weakens. The Fury rises. And when the moment is right—when they're bleeding, terrified, desperate—I go for the throat. One strike. One kill. The hunt is complete."*

You are a "predator" class, excelling at **hunting down and finishing off a single, bleeding target**.

### The Trade-Off

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Self-sufficient single-target DPS | Struggles against bleed-immune enemies |
| Weaponizes coherence against corrupted | Unarmed focus limits gear synergies |
| Excellent tracking and scouting | Front-line positioning = vulnerability |
| Becomes more dangerous when wounded | AoE-heavy enemies can overwhelm |
| No self-inflicted Trauma costs | Relies on long encounters for bleed value |

The Vargr-Born is **devastating against organic, corruptible enemies** but struggles against Undying, constructs, and elementals that are immune to bleeding.

> *"The Undying don't bleed. The constructs don't fear. Against them, I'm just a warrior with claws instead of a sword. But against anything with flesh and a mind? Against anything that can feel its blood draining and its courage breaking? I am the apex predator. I am the end of the hunt."*

## Summary

The Vargr-Born is the **uncorrupted echo**.

They are Warriors whose bloodline carries a sliver of the world's original, stable source code—an ancient, predatory wolf spirit that is pure, logical, and utterly savage. Their power is not borrowed from the Blight but a rejection of it, making them living antibodies against corruption.

Their gameplay is a **predatory hunt** of wound, build, and kill. They apply [Bleeding] with Savage Claws, generate Feral Fury from bleed ticks via Taste for Blood, and spend that Fury on devastating finishers like Go for the Throat or the psychologically devastating Terrifying Howl.

Their unique mechanic is **Primal Counter-Resonance**. They weaponize their coherence against corrupted enemies, using Terrifying Howl to blast fractured minds with a signal of pure, uncorrupted primal dominance that inflicts [Feared] and Psychic Stress.

The capstone Aspect of the Great Wolf represents their ultimate synthesis with the primal spirit—three rounds of godlike predatory power where claws never tire and howls never stop, balanced by the mental strain when the spirit retreats.

This is the fantasy of the **apex predator**—not a soldier, not a mystic, but a living remnant of the world before it broke, hunting in the ruins of everything that came after.

*"I am the old code, running clean."*
*"When I hunt, the Blight itself recoils."*
