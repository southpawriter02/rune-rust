## Pre-Glitch Origins: The Living Air Filter

### Original Purpose

The Lumina-Muscus Pattern Autonomous Atmospheric Purification Organism represented the Vanir Collective's elegant solution to the inevitable byproducts of Aetheric technology—contamination, leakage, and environmental degradation. These bio-mechanical mosses were the breathing lungs of Vanir civilization, quietly absorbing harmful Aetheric emissions and converting them into harmless compounds and gentle light.

| Operational Theater | Function |
|---------------------|----------|

| **Research Laboratories** | Experimental byproduct neutralization; air quality maintenance |
| **Manufacturing Plants** | Industrial emission processing; worker safety enhancement |
| **Deep Infrastructure** | Ventilation system support; sealed environment purification |
| **Public Spaces** | Decorative air purification; aesthetic environmental enhancement |

The Lumina-Muscus was engineered to be the perfect environmental guardian—unobtrusive enough to cover facility walls without interfering with operations, efficient enough to process significant contamination loads, and beautiful enough to enhance rather than diminish living spaces. Its gentle bioluminescence served dual purposes: aesthetic illumination and contamination-level indication. Brighter glow meant more active purification; dim moss meant clean air. It was the invisible shield of Vanir civilization, ensuring that the byproducts of progress never poisoned those who created them.

It was the perfect symbiont, asking nothing but poison and giving back clean air and gentle light.

The Lumina-Muscus Pattern is one of the vanishingly rare examples of Vanir bio-technology that continues to function exactly as designed—because its function required no network oversight to begin with.
