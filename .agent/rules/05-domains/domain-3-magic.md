## Domain 3: Magic System Constraints

### Canonical Ground Truth

**Approved Terminology:**

*   **Aetheric Energy** (fundamental reality substrate)
*   **Runic Blight** / **Blight** (corrupted Aether)
*   **Elder Futhark** (24-rune system)
*   **Galdr** (runic incantation)
*   **Paradox** (reality contradictions)

**Forbidden Terminology:**

*   ❌ "Magic" (generic, non-specific)
*   ❌ "Spell" (D&D-coded)
*   ❌ "Mana" (game-coded)
*   ❌ "Wizard" / "Sorcerer" (generic fantasy)

**Runic System:**

*   Elder Futhark (24 runes) — documented
*   Counter-Rune (Eihwaz ᛇ) — restricted, Apocrypha-level
*   Runes have PRE-GLITCH function + POST-GLITCH corruption

### Audit Checklist

*   \[ \] Zero uses of "magic" terminology?
*   \[ \] Aetheric Energy / Runic Blight terminology used correctly?
*   \[ \] Elder Futhark runes referenced appropriately?
*   \[ \] No generic spell/mana/wizard language?
*   \[ \] Counter-Rune (Eihwaz) restricted to appropriate contexts?
*   \[ \] Runic inscriptions described with canonical mechanics?
*   \[ \] Blight corruption mechanics consistent with Setting Fundamentals?

### Common Violations

❌ "Magic" terminology anywhere
❌ Generic spell/spellcasting language
❌ Mana/wizard/sorcerer terms
❌ Unexplained supernatural effects

### Green Flags

✅ Aetheric Energy terminology
✅ Runic Blight / Blight corruption
✅ Elder Futhark rune references
✅ Galdr incantation terminology
