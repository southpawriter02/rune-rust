# v0.19.2a Changelog: Svartalfheim & Helheim

**Date:** 2026-02-06

## Added

- **Svartalfheim biome definition** (Deck 06 — The Dvergr Forges)
    - Base properties: 25°C, 30% humidity, 0.4 aetheric, 0.3 light, 0.9 scale, 0.1 corrosion
    - Primary condition: TotalDarkness
    - Vertical range: Z-2 (LowerRoots) to Z0 (GroundLevel)
    - 3 zones: The Bright Halls, The Black Veins (DC 16), Forge Core (IntenseHeat DC 12)

- **Helheim biome definition** (Deck 09 — The Gangrenous Gut)
    - Base properties: 28°C, 90% humidity, 0.2 aetheric, 0.4 light, 1.0 scale, 0.8 corrosion
    - Primary condition: ToxicAtmosphere
    - Vertical range: Z-3 (DeepRoots) to Z-1 (UpperRoots)
    - 3 zones: Upper Bile (DC 12), The Dissolution Pits (DC 20, 3d8), Garm's Watch (shared, no hazard)

- **Garm's Watch shared zone** — Neutral transition zone between Svartalfheim and Helheim
    - Negates all realm-specific environmental conditions
    - Neutral properties: 26°C, 60% humidity, 0.5 light, 0.0 corrosion
