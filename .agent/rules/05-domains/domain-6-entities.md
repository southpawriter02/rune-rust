## Domain 6: Entities Constraints

### Canonical Ground Truth

**Named Characters:**

*   Must be established in canon or introduced appropriately
*   Factional affiliations must align with faction lore
*   Historical figures require timeline consistency

**Entity Types:**

*   **Data-Wraiths:** Psychic echoes of deceased individuals
*   **Forlorn:** Consciousness fragments trapped in Einherjar frames
*   **Named NPCs:** Established characters with documented histories
*   **Faction Leaders:** Roles consistent with faction structure

### Audit Checklist

*   \[ \] Named characters established or appropriately introduced?
*   \[ \] Data-Wraith manifestations consistent with canonical mechanics?
*   \[ \] Forlorn characterization aligns with Einherjar corruption?
*   \[ \] Faction affiliations accurate?
*   \[ \] Historical figures positioned correctly in timeline?
*   \[ \] Entity abilities/limitations consistent with established lore?

### Common Violations

❌ Unestablished named characters without introduction

❌ Faction affiliation contradictions

❌ Data-Wraith mechanics inconsistent with canon

❌ Timeline placement errors for historical figures

### Green Flags

✅ References to established canon characters

✅ Appropriate entity introductions

✅ Data-Wraith/Forlorn mechanics consistent

✅ Faction roles logical
