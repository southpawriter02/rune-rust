## Thematic Description

> *"Shun weight that dulls the edge of motion; train breath and steps until evasion is a habit."*
> 

Unencumbered Speed is the foundational philosophy of the Hólmgangr. By **rejecting heavy armor**, you gain the fluidity necessary for dueling—the ability to slip strikes, reposition instantly, and strike before your opponent can react.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Requirement**: Wearing **Light armor or no armor**
- **Defense Bonus**: +2 Defense
- **Movement Bonus**: +1 Movement

### Rank 2

- **Defense Bonus**: +3 Defense (up from +2)
- **Movement Bonus**: +2 Movement (up from +1)
- **PP Cost**: 3

### Rank 3

- **First Strike**: First attack each combat round has **+1 bonus die** to hit
- **PP Cost**: 3

## Tactical Applications

### The Mobile Duelist

1. Wear light armor to maximize bonuses
2. Use +Movement to reach priority targets
3. +Defense compensates for light armor's Soak
4. R3 first-strike advantage sets tempo

### Rank 3: Combat Opener

- +1 bonus die on first attack = higher hit chance
- Opens with Precise Thrust for guaranteed Focus
- Sets the duel's tempo from the start
