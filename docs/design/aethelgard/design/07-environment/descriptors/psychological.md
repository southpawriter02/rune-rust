# Psychological Descriptors — Stress, Trauma, & Corruption

> *"The ruins break bodies. But it's the mind that shatters first."*

---

## 1. Overview

This specification defines descriptors for psychological states — stress accumulation, trauma manifestations, corruption progression, and biome-specific mental pressure.

### 1.1 Identity Table

| Property | Value |

| Category | Trigger | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|

## 2. Stress Descriptors

### 2.1 Stress Thresholds

| Threshold | Range | State |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Minimal | 0-25 | Alert but stable |
| Mounting | 26-50 | Visible strain |
| Critical | 51-75 | Near breaking |
| Breaking | 76-99 | Imminent collapse |
| Broken | 100 | Trauma Check triggered |

### 2.2 Stress Accumulation Descriptors

#### Minimal Stress (0-25)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Your grip tightens on your weapon — unconscious, instinctive" |
| Physical | "A slight headache pulses behind your eyes. Nothing serious. Yet" |
| Mental | "Your thoughts are clear, focused. You can handle this" |
| Mental | "A thread of unease weaves through your awareness, easily ignored" |

#### Mounting Stress (26-50)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Your jaw aches from clenching. You didn't notice you were doing it" |
| Physical | "Sweat breaks out despite the cold. Your body knows something's wrong" |
| Physical | "Your hands won't stay still. They want a weapon, or a way out" |
| Mental | "Shadows seem to move more than they should. You're seeing threats everywhere" |
| Mental | "Thoughts circle, repeat, spiral. Focus slips away like water" |
| Mental | "Every sound makes you flinch. You're wound too tight" |

#### Critical Stress (51-75)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Your heart pounds in your ears, drowning out everything else" |
| Physical | "Hands shake visibly now. Fine motor control is compromised" |
| Physical | "Nausea rises. Your body is rejecting this situation entirely" |
| Mental | "Thoughts fragment. Complete sentences are getting harder" |
| Mental | "The walls feel closer. The ceiling lower. The exit farther" |
| Mental | "You can't remember if you checked that corner. You should check again" |
| Behavioral | "You snap at allies. The words leave before you can stop them" |
| Behavioral | "Every decision feels wrong. Paralysis creeps in" |

#### Breaking Point (76-99)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Your whole body trembles. The shaking won't stop" |
| Physical | "Vision tunnels. The world narrows to a point" |
| Physical | "You can't catch your breath. Lungs refuse to fill properly" |
| Mental | "Thoughts scream, overlap, contradict. Coherence is failing" |
| Mental | "Reality feels thin. Like you could fall through it" |
| Mental | "You're not sure if that's real. You're not sure of anything" |
| Behavioral | "Fight or flight — the only options that make sense now" |
| Behavioral | "You want to RUN. Direction doesn't matter. Just away" |

#### Stress Break (100)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Break | "Something inside you SNAPS. A string pulled too tight, finally giving" |
| Break | "The world tilts. Reality refuses to hold its shape" |
| Break | "You hear yourself screaming. Or is that someone else?" |
| Break | "Control is gone. Your body moves without your permission" |

## 3. Trauma Manifestation Descriptors

### 3.1 Trauma Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Flashbacks** | Unwanted memories, sensory triggers |
| **Panic** | Fight/flight responses, hyperventilation |
| **Dissociation** | Detachment, unreality, depersonalization |
| **Hypervigilance** | Paranoia, startle responses |
| **Avoidance** | Refusal, frozen, unable to proceed |

### 3.2 Flashback Descriptors

| Intensity | Descriptor |
|-----------|------------|
| Mild | "A memory surfaces, unwanted. You push it back down" |
| Mild | "For a moment, you're somewhere else. Someone else. Then it passes" |
| Moderate | "The past bleeds into the present. You see both at once" |
| Moderate | "That sound — you've heard it before. Your body remembers before your mind does" |
| Severe | "You're there again. Not here. THERE. Where it happened" |
| Severe | "The memory has teeth. It's dragging you under and you can't stop it" |

### 3.3 Panic Descriptors

| Intensity | Descriptor |
|-----------|------------|
| Mild | "Your heart skips. A lurch of fear, quickly controlled" |
| Mild | "Adrenaline surges, looking for danger that may not exist" |
| Moderate | "Panic claws at your chest. Breathing becomes a conscious effort" |
| Moderate | "Every instinct screams RUN but there's nowhere to go" |
| Severe | "You can't breathe. You can't think. You can only feel the terror" |
| Severe | "Your body has taken over. It's running, fighting, or freezing — you don't choose" |

### 3.4 Dissociation Descriptors

| Intensity | Descriptor |
|-----------|------------|
| Mild | "For a moment, you feel distant from your body. A step removed" |
| Mild | "Your hands move but they don't feel like your hands" |
| Moderate | "You're watching yourself from outside. A puppet without a script" |
| Moderate | "The world has gone flat. Two-dimensional. Unreal" |
| Severe | "You're not sure who you are. The name feels like a word, not an identity" |
| Severe | "Reality is a stage. You're watching the play. None of it is real" |

### 3.5 Hypervigilance Descriptors

| Intensity | Descriptor |
|-----------|------------|
| Mild | "You're watching the shadows closer than you need to" |
| Mild | "Every exit is noted. Every potential weapon catalogued" |
| Moderate | "You can't relax. Your body refuses. There's danger everywhere" |
| Moderate | "The slightest sound makes you spin, weapon raised" |
| Severe | "Everyone is a threat. Everything is a trap. Trust is impossible" |
| Severe | "You haven't slept in days. Can't afford to. They're out there" |

## 4. Corruption Descriptors

### 4.1 Corruption Thresholds

| Threshold | Range | State | Physical |
|-----------|-------|-------|----------|
| Clean | 0-10 | Uncorrupted | None visible |
| Touched | 11-30 | Minor corruption | Subtle changes |
| Tainted | 31-50 | Noticeable corruption | Visible marks |
| Corrupted | 51-75 | Significant corruption | Transformation begins |
| Lost | 76-99 | Near-Forlorn | Major physical changes |
| Forlorn | 100 | Transformation | Character lost |

### 4.2 Corruption Accumulation Descriptors

#### Clean (0-10)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Self | "No marks. No stains. But you've seen what you might become" |
| Self | "Clean, for now. Every ruin visited adds to the inevitable" |

#### Touched (11-30)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "A paleness that doesn't wash away. A coldness that doesn't warm" |
| Physical | "Your reflection looks... different. Subtly wrong" |
| Mental | "Dreams that aren't yours. Memories that never happened" |
| Mental | "Sometimes you hear the whispers. Just below understanding" |

#### Tainted (31-50)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Veins show through the skin, darker than they should be" |
| Physical | "Your shadow doesn't always match your movements" |
| Physical | "A mark has appeared. It wasn't there yesterday. It won't come off" |
| Mental | "The whispers have words now. You're trying not to understand them" |
| Mental | "You're forgetting things. Small things. Important things" |
| Social | "People look at you differently. They sense what you're becoming" |

#### Corrupted (51-75)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Your skin has changed texture. Not quite human anymore" |
| Physical | "One eye doesn't blink the same as the other" |
| Physical | "You move differently. Your body follows new rules now" |
| Mental | "The whispers have become a voice. It knows your name" |
| Mental | "Memory is becoming... optional. You choose what to remember" |
| Mental | "The hunger is new. You don't want to think about what it wants" |
| Social | "Allies keep their distance. They're right to" |

#### Lost (76-99)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Physical | "Looking in mirrors is inadvisable. What looks back isn't entirely you" |
| Physical | "Your body obeys, but reluctantly. It has its own agenda now" |
| Physical | "The transformation is visible. Denial is no longer possible" |
| Mental | "You're losing yourself. Watching it happen. Unable to stop it" |
| Mental | "The voice is louder than your thoughts. It might be winning" |
| Urgent | "There's still time. Maybe. If you can find help. If anyone will help you" |

#### Forlorn (100)

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Final | "The change is complete. What was you is now... other" |
| Final | "You fall. What rises wearing your face is no longer you" |
| Final | "The last thing you see is your own hands, becoming something else" |

## 6. Recovery Descriptors

### 6.1 Stress Recovery

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Rest | "Breathing slows. The shaking stops. You're still here" |
| Rest | "Silence, but the good kind. The safe kind" |
| Social | "Another voice. Human. Not threatening. You'd forgotten the sound" |
| Social | "They're watching your back. You can lower your guard, just a little" |
| Milestone | "You survived. Again. It doesn't feel like victory, but it is" |

### 6.2 Trauma Management

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|
| Grounding | "Count the exits. Name the objects. You're here. You're now. You're safe" |
| Grounding | "Breathe. Just breathe. The memory is then. This is now" |

| Support | "You don't have to explain. They know. They survived it too" |

| Acceptance | "You carry it. But you don't have to carry it alone" |

> Corruption is permanent. It does not decrease. These descriptors are for acceptance, not reversal.

| Type | Descriptor |
|------|------------|

| Bargaining | "Maybe there's a cure. Maybe the Dvergr know something. Maybe..." |
