## The Yggdrasil Super-Arcology: Vertical Structure

The Nine Realms are **not separate dimensions**—they are vertically stacked "Decks" within a single, continent-sized super-arcology called **Project Yggdrasil**.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/META-The-Master-Gazetteer-of-the-Nine-Realms-28955eb312da8008bc22cd8fc8ece993?pvs=21)[[2]](https://www.notion.so/The-Yggdrasil-Network-The-Nine-Realms-28255eb312da80e0ae63ee586fa28a12?pvs=21)

## Inter-Realm Connections (Post-Glitch)

### Functional Transit Routes

| Route | Method | Travel Time | Classification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Svartalfheim ⇆ Jötunheim** | Deep Gates (Nidavellir-Utgard) | 180 km in 12 minutes | A-class |
| **Svartalfheim ⇆ Helheim** | Garm's Watch elevator shaft | Variable | B-class (sealed) |
| **Midgard ⇆ Jötunheim** | Utgard Gates via Crossroads | 5-8 days | B/C-class |
| **Midgard ⇆ Vanaheim** | Ferry up-river | 6-9 days | B/C-class |
| **Midgard ⇆ Niflheim** | Ridge routes to Dreadnought corridors | 7-12 days | B/D-class |
| **Midgard ⇆ Asgard** | Scar Rim lifts to orbital structure | 3-5 days | B-class |

### Critical Connection Details

- **Deep Gates**: Only 10% remain functional—all maintained by Dvergr using mechanical (non-Aetheric) overrides[[3]](https://www.notion.so/The-Deep-A-Geographic-Framework-of-Subterranean-Aethelgard-a6340b938e3341dca72be6e8e7bc012d?pvs=21)
- **Garm's Watch**: Only breathable air in Helheim comes from Svartalfheim atmospheric supply[[4]](https://www.notion.so/Helheim-The-Gangrenous-Gut-c9a63798eb374e5b811a6f73eb294d5f?pvs=21)
- **Asgardian Scar**: Portions of Asgard crashed into Midgard during the Glitch—the main structure remains in degraded orbit[[5]](https://www.notion.so/Midgard-The-Tamed-Ruin-8f0e05b2472848d7b9dc2fb562d3434c?pvs=21)
- **Helheim → Midgard**: Toxic contamination flows via subterranean aquifers (Souring Mires)[[4]](https://www.notion.so/Helheim-The-Gangrenous-Gut-c9a63798eb374e5b811a6f73eb294d5f?pvs=21)

## Key Structural Relationships

### Vertical Dependencies

- **Muspelheim** (heat) + **Niflheim** (cold) = Thermal regulation system → Their combined failure created the Fimbulwinter[[6]](https://www.notion.so/Niflheim-The-Frozen-Tomb-22258458f78d43a2b585e6f5d4dd2907?pvs=21)
- **Svartalfheim** supplied raw materials → **Jötunheim** assembled them → Products went up to **Midgard**
- **Helheim** processed waste from all realms above → Failure poisons everything via aquifer dispersal

### The Asgard Crash

- **Orbital Asgard** remains in degraded orbit (Deck 01)
- **Asgardian Scar** in Midgard is the impact crater where portions crashed during the Glitch
- The Scar is Midgard territory, but accessing orbital Asgard requires Scar Rim lifts[[5]](https://www.notion.so/Midgard-The-Tamed-Ruin-8f0e05b2472848d7b9dc2fb562d3434c?pvs=21)

### Alfheim's Position

- Canonically "hidden atop Vanaheim or connected to Asgard"[[7]](https://www.notion.so/Aethelgard-Setting-Compliance-Guide-2ba55eb312da8014b792e98207480e2b?pvs=21)
- Shares ancestral link with Svartalfheim (both descended from Deck researchers)
- Conceptually adjacent to Asgard (shared cognitive hazard environment)[[8]](https://www.notion.so/Alfheim-The-Supercomputer-of-Madness-4512f5a9ad8143b7a802488899d4d292?pvs=21)
