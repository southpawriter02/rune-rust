## Domain 5: Species Constraints

### Canonical Ground Truth

**Species Documentation:**

*   Codex databases define canonical species
*   Fauna & Flora Codex = authoritative source
*   No unauthorized species additions
*   Blight-mutated variants must reference baseline species

**Species Categories:**

*   **Baseline Species:** Natural pre-Glitch organisms
*   **Blight-Mutated:** Post-Glitch corrupted variants
*   **Bio-Engineered:** Vanir/Aesir designed organisms (pre-Glitch)
*   **Automata:** Mechanoid entities (Forlorn, Assembly-Line Golems)

### Audit Checklist

*   \[ \] All fauna/flora referenced in Codex or established canon?
*   \[ \] Blight-mutated species reference canonical baseline?
*   \[ \] Bio-engineered species have PRE-GLITCH origin documentation?
*   \[ \] No unauthorized species additions?
*   \[ \] Species biology consistent with established mechanics?
*   \[ \] Evolutionary/mutation mechanics align with Gene-Storm/Blight lore?

### Common Violations

❌ Undocumented species (no Codex entry or canon reference)
❌ Biological contradictions (abilities inconsistent with lore)
❌ Unexplained species origins

### Green Flags

✅ References to documented Codex species
✅ Blight-mutation mechanics consistent
✅ Bio-engineering traced to Vanir/Aesir
✅ Evolutionary lineages logical
