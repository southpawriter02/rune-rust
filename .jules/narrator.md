# The Narrator's Journal

## 2024-05-22 - Voice Calibration
**Challenge:** Modern technical terms in legacy content (Hydraulic, Reactor, Ozone).
**Translation:**
- Hydraulic -> Fluid-Muscle / Blood-Pipe
- Reactor -> Heart-Engine / Sun-Shard
- Ozone -> Storm-Scorch / Lightning-Smell
- Robot -> Iron-Walker / Automaton / Husk
- Concrete -> False-Stone / Grey-Rock
**Why:** To enforce the "Ignorance is Aesthetic" principle. Characters see the symptom (movement, heat, smell), not the mechanism.

**Challenge:** "Expert" Detail Level in Bestiary.
**Translation:** Shift from "Technical Manual" to "Survivor's Wisdom".
**Why:** Even an expert in Aethelgard doesn't know *how* a CPU works. They know "If you hit the glowing glass, the spirit dies."
