# The Worldbuilder's Journal

## 2024-05-22 - Atgeir-Wielder Migration
**Design Constraint:** Legacy dice notation (`4d6` in Line Breaker) violated the Tiered Damage Hierarchy (d4-d10).
**Solution:** Converted damage to `3d8` to fit the Tier 2 power curve while maintaining average damage output closer to the intended design without using forbidden d6 clusters.
