## I. Purpose

v0.38.13 creates **Ambient Environmental Descriptors**, making the world feel alive:

- **60+ Ambient Sound Descriptors** (per biome/location type)
- **40+ Smell Descriptors** (environmental, creature, decay)
- **30+ Atmospheric Detail Descriptors** (air quality, weather effects)
- **20+ Background Activity Descriptors** (distant events)

**Strategic Function:**

Currently, environments are silent and sterile:

- ❌ "You enter a corridor."
- ❌ No ambient sounds or smells
- ❌ World feels dead

**v0.38.13 Solution:**

- Contextual ambient sounds that fire periodically
- Smell descriptions that reinforce biome atmosphere
- Background activity that suggests a larger world
- Day/night atmospheric variations

## II. Ambient Sounds by Biome

### The Roots (Corroded Infrastructure)

### Mechanical Sounds

**Active Areas:**

- "The distant *thrum* of still-functioning machinery echoes through the halls."
- "Hydraulic systems hiss and groan somewhere in the walls."
- "A rhythmic *clank-clank-clank* of ancient pumps keeping the lower levels dry."
- "Steam vents periodically with a sharp *hiss*."
- "You hear the whir of a Servitor passing through a distant corridor."

**Decay Sounds:**

- "Metal creaks ominously overhead, rust giving way slowly."
- "Water drips steadily from corroded pipes. *Plink. Plink. Plink.*"
- "A distant crash as something structural finally gives way."
- "The groan of stressed metal—this place is falling apart."
- "Rust flakes rain down from the ceiling with a soft *patter*."

**Eerie Quiet:**

- "The silence is oppressive. Even your footsteps seem too loud."
- "Nothing. Just the sound of your own breathing and the thud of your heartbeat."
- "The air is still. Too still."

### Creature Sounds

- "Tiny claws skitter across metal somewhere in the walls."

**Distant Threats:**

- "A hollow moan echoes from somewhere deep below. Forlorn."
- "The metallic scrape of Servitor limbs on stone, far away but getting closer."
