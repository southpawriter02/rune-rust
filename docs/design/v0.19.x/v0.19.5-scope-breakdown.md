## Cognitive Hazard Mechanics

### RealityFlux Effects (Alfheim)

- **Base DC:** 12
- **Damage:** Stress accumulation (not HP)
- **Frequency:** Per 10 minutes
- **Effects:**
    - Navigation becomes unreliable (random redirection)
    - Visual hallucinations
    - Memory distortion (forget recent events)
    - Genius Loci may manifest
- **Mitigations:** Aetheric dampeners, LjÃ³sÃ¡lfar ancestry, Stress resistance

### CpsExposure Effects (Asgard)

- **Base DC:** 14
- **Damage:** CPS (Cognitive Paradox Syndrome) accumulation
- **Frequency:** Per Hour
- **Effects:**
    - Paradox points accumulate
    - At thresholds: dÃ©jÃ  vu, time perception distortion, temporal loops
    - Severe: cognitive breakdown
- **Mitigations:** CPS suppressants, Ã†sir ancestry, temporal anchoring

### Genius Loci (Alfheim Special)

- Entities born from concentrated aetheric energy
- Appear when RealityFlux check fails by 10+
- Not hostile by default, but reality warps around them
- Can provide information or transportation if appeased

### Heimdallr Signal (Asgard Special)

- Automated broadcast from Deck 01 observatory
- Triggers when entering Heimdallr's Platform
- Contains corrupted surveillance data
- Provides cryptic clues but causes CPS spike
