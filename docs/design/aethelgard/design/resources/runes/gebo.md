When invoked now, Gebō's command to "balance the exchange" is processed by the chaotic Aether—a system that interprets "fairness" through paradoxical, alien logic.

### The Corrupted Imperative

The Ginnungagap Glitch transformed Gebō from a precise transaction tool into a chaotic engine of cosmic rebalancing. Without O.D.I.N. to arbitrate exchanges and verify intent, Gebō's "balance" command interprets fairness through the Blight's anti-logic—often with catastrophic results.

**The Monkey's Paw Phenomenon:**

> *"The corruption of Gebō is the corruption of the very concept of a fair deal. When invoked, its attempt to create a 'balance' is processed by the chaotic Aether, which no longer understands the difference between a copper coin and a cherished memory, between a trade good and a soul's weight of grief. A spell to create a protective ward by drawing energy from the earth may instead drain the caster's own life-force to 'pay' the ground. The 'gift' of the rune is now almost always a monkey's paw—technically fair by some alien calculus, but catastrophically exceeding any reasonable interpretation of the original agreement."*
