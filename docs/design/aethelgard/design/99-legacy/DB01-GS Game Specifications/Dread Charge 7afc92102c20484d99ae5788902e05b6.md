## Thematic Description

> *"A fierce cry and a blur forward from back rank to front; presence alone rattles frayed minds."*
> 

Dread Charge is the Strandhögg's **gap-closer and disruptor**. You burst from concealment or the back line, your sudden violence disorient enemies before they can react.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Cost**: 35 Stamina
- **Movement**: Move up to **4 tiles**
- **Attack**: FINESSE vs target's Defense
- **Damage**: 2d10 Physical
- **Save Effect**: Target must make **WILL check DC 12** or become **[Disoriented]** for 1 round

### Rank 2

- **Movement**: Up to **6 tiles** (up from 4)
- **Save DC**: 14 (up from 12)
- **PP Cost**: 3

### Rank 3

- **Pace Generation**: On successful Disorientation, generate **20 Pace**
- **PP Cost**: 3

---

## Status Effect: [Disoriented]

**Duration**: 1 round

**Effects**:

- -2 penalty to attack rolls
- Cannot take Reactions
- Disadvantage on Perception checks

**Enables**:

- Reaver's Strike R3 bonus damage
- Vicious Flank base requirement
- Savage Harvest easier to land

## Synergies

### Internal (Strandhögg Tree)

- **Harrier's Alacrity**: High Initiative + Dread Charge = first-turn disruption
- **Reaver's Strike**: [Disoriented] enables R3 bonus
- **Tidal Rush**: Disorientation triggers Pace generation

### External (Party Composition)

- **Controllers**: Stack debuffs for Vicious Flank
- **Ranged specialists**: Engage while allies fire
- **Ambush teams**: Coordinated first strikes

## Tactical Applications

### The Alpha Opener

1. Combat begins: High Initiative (Harrier's Alacrity)
2. Dread Charge from concealment (4-6 tiles)
3. Target [Disoriented] → Tidal Rush → +Pace
4. R3: +20 Pace on disorientation
5. Follow-up attacks have advantage

### Pace Economy (Rank 3)

- Movement: +20-30 Pace (4-6 tiles × 5)
- Disorientation: +20 Pace
- Potential single action: +50 Pace
