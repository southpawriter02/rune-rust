## Core Identity

You draw an arrow tipped with toxin harvested from Blighted flora—a calculated application of the world's own sickness. The toxin is unstable, reactive, designed to **exploit the target's existing corruption**. You're turning the Blight against itself.

---

## Mechanics

### Cost

25 Stamina | **Standard Action** | **Ranged Attack**

### Effect

- FINESSE-based ranged attack
- **Immediate Damage:** 2d8 + FINESSE Physical damage
- **Apply [Blighted Toxin] debuff:**
    - **DoT:** 1d6 damage per turn for 3 turns
    - **Corruption Exploit:** If target has High or Extreme Runic Blight Corruption: 30% chance to trigger [Disoriented] (1 turn)
    - **[Disoriented]:** Target suffers -2 dice penalty to all actions

### Rank Progression

| Rank | PP Cost | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 4 | Base effects, 30% [Glitch] chance vs corrupted |
| 2 | 20 | 4d6 toxin damage, 4 turns, 60% [Glitch] chance |
| 3 | Capstone | 3d6/turn toxin, 80% [Glitch] chance |

---

## Tactical Applications

**Corruption Synergy:**

- Low Corruption targets: Just the DoT
- High/Extreme Corruption targets: DoT + system disruption

**Synergies:**

- **Exploit Corruption:** Amplifies effectiveness against same target type
- **Mark for Death:** Amplifies the immediate damage portion
- **Heartseeker Shot:** Benefits from target already being corrupted
