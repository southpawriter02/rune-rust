## Thematic Description

> *"A spare motion to a named point; tests guard and trims accuracy of the foe that answers poorly."*
> 

Precise Thrust is the Hólmgangr's **core melee attack**. A clean, economical strike that builds Focus against your marked opponent—the foundation of your dueling rhythm.

---

## Mechanical Implementation

### Base Effect (Rank 1)

- **Cost**: 30 Stamina
- **Target**: Single enemy (melee range)
- **Attack**: FINESSE vs target's Defense
- **Damage**: 2d8 Physical
- **Focus Generation**: If target is your [Dueling Target], generate **10 Focus**

### Rank 2

- **Damage**: 3d8 Physical (up from 2d8)
- **Focus Generation**: 15 Focus vs Dueling Target (up from 10)
- **PP Cost**: 3

### Rank 3

- **Debuff**: On hit, target suffers **-1 die** to their next attack against you
- **PP Cost**: 3

## Synergies

### Internal (Hólmgangr Tree)

- **Challenge of Honour**: Mark target first for Focus generation
- **Singular Focus**: Consecutive hits stack damage bonus
- **Exploit Opening**: Build Focus to enable finisher

### External (Party Composition)

- **Controllers**: Debuffed target easier to CC
- **Follow-up strikers**: -1 die makes target vulnerable
- **Skald**: Tempo buffs enhance attack frequency

---

## Tactical Applications

### Focus Building Loop

1. Challenge of Honour (mark target)
2. Precise Thrust (+10/15 Focus)
3. Reactive Parry (+15 Focus on counter)
4. Repeat until Focus threshold reached
5. Execute with finisher ability

### Rank 3: Defensive Value

- -1 die to target's next attack
- Stacks with Unencumbered Speed's +Defense
- Makes the duel's rhythm favor you
